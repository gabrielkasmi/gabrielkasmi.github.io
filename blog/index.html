What Does a Solar Panel Detector Actually See?

July 2026 Explainable AI Computer Vision

While developing DeepPVMapper, the recurring question was never how accurate the model was, but whether it was actually detecting solar panels. Answering that turned out to require a new way of explaining models — decomposing predictions into wavelet scales — and revealed that the model was, in fact, an excellent grid detector.
