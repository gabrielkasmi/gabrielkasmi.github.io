Welcome

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at Mines Paris - PSL. I prepared my PhD thesis at Mines Paris (OIE Center) under the supervision of Philippe Blanc, Yves-Marie Saint-Drenan and Laurent Dubus. This PhD was funded by RTE France, the French transmission system operator (TSO), and benefited from the support of the ANRT (CIFRE funding). Click here for an overview of the work done in the PhD.

My research focuses on the reliability and transparency of deep learning algorithms deployed in industrial settings. My research interests are explainable AI, computer vision and remote sensing. My main case study is the remote sensing of rooftop photovoltaic installations, so I also made contributions in power systems.
