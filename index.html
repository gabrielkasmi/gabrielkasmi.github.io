About me

I'm currently a PhD Student at the Observations, Impacts, Energie (O.I.E.) center at Mines Paris - PSL University . My PhD is carried out under a CIFRE contract funded by RTE France, the French transmission system operator. I work under the supervision of Philippe Blanc, Yves-Marie Saint-Drenan and Laurent Dubus.

My research interests revolve around machine learning and computer vision, with an emphasis on robustness to distribution shifts that can occur in practical applications. I tackle this issue from a practical point of view ; trying to find methods that can improve the reliability of deep learning-based computer vision models. I'm also interested in remote sensing and PV energy. You can access my research papers here.

I graduated from ENSAE Paris and Ecole normale supérieure Paris-Saclay as normalien (civil servant).
